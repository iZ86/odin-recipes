Beef Ramen Noodles

Description

Packed with tender steak and a rich, beefy broth for plenty of protein. It's a delicious way to get a vitamin and mineral boost too, thanks to fresh ingredients like pak choi, spring onions and ginger.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. 100% Coconut Oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (very finely chopped)
  • Cube of ginger (very finely chopped)
  • 1 chilli (finely chopped)
  • 3 spring onions (roughly chopped)
  • 1 litre beef stock (use two stock cubes)
  • 250 ml boiling water
  • 3 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp. five spice
  • 4 pak choi leaves
  • 2 packs instant ramen noodles
  • 1 tsp. 100% Coconut Oil
  • 1 8oz sirloin steak
  • 3 eggs (1 per serving)
    • To garnish
  • Chopped spring onion
  • Coriander
